The Dr. Roto-scope
Special contribution from Dr. Roto
His name is Mark Bloom, but you can call him Dr. Roto. He’s an expert in his field, and his field just so happens to be fantasy sports. He’s a senior fantasy sports analyst for Scout, where you can watch him on Scout Fantasy TV. You can also hear him daily on SiriusXM Fantasy Sports Radio (channel 87).

In 2014, Dr. Roto gave 500 contestants a chance to take him down a peg in his Beat Dr. Roto contest. None of them succeeded. Back in 2011, he won the Fantasy Sports Writers Association’s Newcomer of the Year award—yes, that’s a thing. He’s won a ton of leagues throughout the years, has been playing fantasy sports since the 1990s, and is simply way too good to be grading the JADDL Draft.

Yet here he is, grading the 2015 JADDL Draft. Dr. Roto was gracious enough to give us his two best and two worst selections, as well as name a winner of the Torn Hoodie.

The Worst
Red Hornets
Palmer and Mariota? Really?? Julius Thomas being out for a month hurts too. Oh, and T.Y. and Gurley are injured, too. This would be a great team … in a dynasty keeper league … but not this year.

Millennium Falcons
I love Star Wars, but I think that this team might be facing the Dark Side—VJax, Colston, and Fred?? Old is not good. Alf Morris might lose his job too at some point, and [Ian] does not own Matt Jones. He will need The Force to survive this year.

The Best
Team Odouls
Calvin, Cooks, and Andre to go with Big Ben is a great start. If Arian comes back quickly, and if Cameron Artis-Payne gets the starting running back job, [Adam] will be someone I do not want to see in the playoffs.

The Mighty Boom
Looks like Boom is going to dominate this season. I love Dez, Julio Jones Roto, and Allen Robinson. His WRs are VISIONARY!! He has Murray, Ivory, and Abdullah too. He needs a TE when Gates comes back, but otherwise he looks tough to beat. Boom is my pick for the best draft.
