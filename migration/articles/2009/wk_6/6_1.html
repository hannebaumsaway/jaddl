Brady’s performance was very much needed for the Flying Pigs (2-4) to emerge victorious. The game also marked Hauloll’s first loss at home this season.

“I made my fortune caring for old people,” commented Pigs owner Jacob, manager of the nation’s most renowned retirement community. “My charges include Kurt Warner and Brett Favre in the off-season. You know who my charges don’t include? Tom mother-fucking Brady.”
