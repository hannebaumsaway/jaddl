The Return of Ron Mexico waltzed into L-town in search of its first victory, but crawled out with a bruised forehead instead after being teabagged by Chris Johnson and The Mighty Boom (2-0) to the tune of 132.9 - 92.7.

52.4 fucking points. I say again: holy shit.
