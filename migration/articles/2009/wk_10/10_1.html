Peterson gives O fans O-faces
Team Odouls is winning again. Yawn. They’re in solid playoff position again. Yawn. They’ve scored the most points in the league to this point, yet have underachieved record-wise…again.

Yawn.

But what Odouls RB Adrian Peterson does on the field is anything but boring. It’s so un-boring, in fact, it’s practically X-rated.

Peterson’s jukes and bursts of speed on the field are so electrifying, women in the crowd have a difficult time…uh…containing themselves. This phenomenon has become so prevalent that parents have become wary of bringing their children to Odouls games.

Team owner Adam dismisses the orgy-like atmosphere as a financial detriment, however.

“We haven’t noticed a drop-off in ticket sales at all,” said Adam. “For any family that chooses to stay home, a throng of young women is chomping at the bit to take their seats. In fact, all this has done is created a more intimidating environment for our opponents. Sixty thousand screaming women—it’s like a sorority convention on crack.”

Ticket revenue may not be a problem for Adam, but it’s possible TV revenue may soon be. The Federal Communications Commission has expressed concerns about showing the Odouls “O-Fest” on the air.

“JADDL games are supposed to be family programming,” said an FCC spokesperson. “There is nothing familial about the disgusting sexuality displayed in that stadium every weekend.”

The FCC hasn’t announced any planned action just yet, but the spokesperson wouldn’t rule out the possibility of blacking out Odouls’ remaining home games.

“We will do whatever it takes to keep America safe from terrorism and sex.”

One thing is for sure: Adam won’t be asking Peterson to tone down the moves anytime soon, seeing as how those moves have accounted for 16.4% of Odouls’ scoring to this point, or 19.1 points per game—behind only Chris Johnson and Maurice Jones-Drew among RBs in the league. That includes 22.3 points in a 139.7–65.1 clowning of the Millennium Falcons this past weekend.

“Adrian fuels our team,” said Adam. “If he happens to rev the engines of the housewives in Houston while he’s at it, all the better. You should see the sales figures for our women’s pink number 28 jerseys.”
