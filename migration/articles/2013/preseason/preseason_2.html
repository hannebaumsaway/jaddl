The Court-Ordered Limousine was finally ordered
As your commissioner, I want to apologize. For a league as prestigious and proud as this one, an unacceptable situation has existed the past six years—a situation which was completely rectifiable.
You’ll just have to go out and win the damn thing for yourself in December.

We haven’t had a real championship trophy.

Well, I’m happy to tell you that this travesty has been resolved. Thanks to a couple nice fellows on eBay and the fine folks at Midwest Trophy & Engraving, we now have a true Court-Ordered Limousine worthy of its standing as our league’s championship hardware.

If you want to know what the trophy is like in person, you’ll have to ask Ian, as it now rests in its (current) rightful place at his abode. Or, you’ll just have to go out and win the damn thing for yourself in December.

I want to personally thank Alex, whose ambition and creativity with the Eastern Goblet—not to mention the recommendation of Midwest Trophy—led directly to me getting off my ass and getting the Limo done after all these years.

Fight hard out there, boys…you’re actually playing for something other than money from now on.
