“F” draft grade? F that
Well, Jake Gutierrez, looks like the One Pump Chumps aren’t going to simply sit back and let you F them in the A with your F rating.

After it looked like the Chumps would do just that in Week 1, posting the league’s lowest score, they rebounded with a vengeance in Week 2, taking home the Briefly Badass award with a 132.1–77.6 F’ing of the scuffling Red Hornets. The scoring firm of Jackson & Jackson led the way for OPC; WR Vincent Jackson with 29.2, followed closely by RB Fred Jackson with 28.

Former Mizzou-rah standout Jeremy Maclin did his best to put the sting back in the Hornets (0-2) by matching Vincent Jackson’s 29.2-point output, and Tony “Hey look, I’m kinda hurt again!” Romo crawled heroically back onto the field and threw up 23.8, but that’s where the good news ends for the ’Nets. Austin Collie, with a Kerry Collins-shaped albatross around his neck, only managed 2.4, which was hilariously 4.2 points more than Ricky Williams scored.

Scored? More like deducted. And he wasn’t alone. The Bills D/ST surpassed Slicky Ricky’s output—in the wrong direction—with -3.
