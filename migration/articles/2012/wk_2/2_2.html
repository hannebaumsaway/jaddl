PANDEMONIUM!
This must be the Bizarro JADDL…
When you’re finished reading this article, click here. Scroll down a bit and you’ll see all the franchises of the JADDL, stacked up in descending order by prestigiousness.

Keep scrolling and you’ll eventually spot the Tulsa Angry Monkeys, IPA Connoisseur, and Lawrence Football Jesus…just above the four inactive former franchises listed for posterity’s sake.

In case you weren’t following along just then, I’m pointing out to you that the Monkeys, IPA, and Football Jesus are the three least prestigious teams in the league today. Those three franchises combine for a .396 winning percentage, four total playoff appearances, and just two trophies (both Torn Hoodies, which is the only award not actually earned on the imaginary field) that aren’t Briefly Badasses. This bleak trio also collectively holds zero league records to date.

Have I made my point?

Good. Because at this point in the young season you can throw all that shit out the imaginary window.

Two weeks. Six wins. No losses. And so far their performances don’t appear to be fluky. The Connoisseur has certainly earned their standing. Football Jesus is second in the East in scoring. The Monkeys have had a more fortunate go of things than the other two, averaging just a 10.5-point margin of victory in their two wins—IPA’s mark is 43.9, while Jesus’ is 28.9.

This is all meaningless so early in the season, of course, but it’ll be interesting to follow nonetheless. The Monkeys and IPA face off next week, so one of those two will be hampered with their first loss in short order. On the right-hand side of the JADDL world, Jesus’ path seems more clear thus far, with the two-time defending division champ Bensons struggling out of the gate. Then again, there’s always The Mighty Boom to contend with, who have scored more points than Jesus in the first two weeks and against whom Jesus is 2-4-1 lifetime.

At any rate, these teams don’t get called out for something good very often, so it’s nice to be able to write this piece. Like spare change to a homeless guy, the first two weeks of 2012 have likely breathed disproportionate life into three franchises for whom winning is a delicacy to be savored while it lasts.
