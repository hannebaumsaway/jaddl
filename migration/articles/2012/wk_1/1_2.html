Miss the Fifth Reich’s picks? You’re half in luck
One of the most ballsy things you can do in the sports world—you know, aside from actually playing the sport—is betting a bunch of money on a game.

One of the least ballsy things you can do in the sports world is make a consequence-free bet on the outcome of a fantasy game.

But fuck it, it’s fun anyway. Such is the ideal that is bringing back the Fifth Reich’s Picks…with a twist. Starting with Week 2 (because starting in Week 1 would have just been too on-the-nose), John Jordan and his dusty crystal ball will make their triumphant return.

Not only is the picks segment coming back; it’ll be more nimble than ever, thanks to the fact that it has moved to The DDL. This takes the task of formatting emails or Word documents for the newsletter out of the picture, getting you the picks you crave even faster than before.

But wait! There’s more! The picks are going to be presented in a new, sexy way via The Program That Will Never Die: MS Paint. That’s right: John and Alex are bringing visual aids up in this bitch.

There’s one more change to announce. John will be returning to pick only the games in the East Division.

Why?

Because the picks for the West Division games will be handled by the Bad News Bensons’ front office, that’s why. You see, that way Alex can avoid picking games involving his own franchise. Brilliant!
