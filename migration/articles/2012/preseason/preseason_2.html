Changes to the JADDL website
If your JADDL obsession tips the scale, you may have already noticed some additions to this site in the off-season.

Assuming you haven’t, here’s a little cheatsheet to help you uncover the new wonders the site has to behold:

  • Article page navigation. I’m talking about the little arrows above the articles. Now you can traverse from week to week within the news articles without resorting back to the site navigation bar’s menus. Handy, huh?

  • Franchise carousel. That jumpy little bar of team logos that now hovers above the main body of the site, giving you quick access to a team’s bio page from anywhere on the site.

  • Records page. A convenient place for you to gather talking points as you prepare to talk shit on a division rival, perhaps, or maybe just a way for you to brush up on your general JADDL knowledge to wow fans at a cocktail party.

  • Article listings on team bio pages. Have you ever wished you could see all the articles in which your team was featured or mentioned? Now you can! Just scroll down to the bottom of your team’s bio page and bask in the glory of all your media coverage. Of course, not all of your mentions are going to be glowing…
